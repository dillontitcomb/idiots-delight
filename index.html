Welcome to Idiot's Delight

A game for proper intellectuals

The rules of the game are quite simple:

  • If two cards are the same suit, the lower value card can be removed.
  • If a stack of cards is empty, any card can be moved to that stack.
  • To move a card to an empty stack, click the empty stack, then click the card you want to move.
  • If you are out of options, click DEAL to get four new cards.
